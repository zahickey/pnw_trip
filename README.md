# PNW → Alaska Road Trip

A month-long drive from San Diego to Hyder, Alaska, in a 1998 Six-Pac 150 truck camper on a 2020 Ford Ranger. Climbing, national parks, and beautiful hikes the whole way up.

**Dates:** July 22 – August 15, 2026 (25 days)
**Route:** San Diego → Sierra Nevada → Pacific Northwest → BC → Hyder, AK (via the Cassiar Highway — no ferry required)

## The route at a glance

- **8 national parks:** Joshua Tree, Yosemite, Crater Lake, Mt. Rainier, Olympic, North Cascades, Banff, Jasper
- **Climbing anchors:** Joshua Tree and Yosemite (a beginner-friendly top-rope-to-multi-pitch progression, traveling with an experienced climber), plus Smith Rock and Squamish
- **Trip end:** Hyder, AK — reached entirely by road via the Cassiar Highway, no ferry needed. Salmon Glacier + grizzly viewing at Fish Creek, with two nights to enjoy it.
