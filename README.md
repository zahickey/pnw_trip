# PNW → Alaska Road Trip

A month-long drive from San Diego to Ketchikan, Alaska, in a 1998 Six-Pac 150 truck camper on a 2020 Ford Ranger. Climbing, national parks, and beautiful hikes the whole way up.

**Dates:** July 20 – August 14, 2026 (26 days)
**Route:** San Diego → Sierra Nevada → Pacific Northwest → BC → Southeast Alaska (via ferry from Prince Rupert)

## The route at a glance

- **9 national parks:** Joshua Tree, Yosemite, Crater Lake, Mt. Rainier, Olympic, North Cascades, Banff, Jasper
- **Climbing anchors:** Joshua Tree, Yosemite (Tuolumne), Smith Rock, Squamish
- **Bonus stop:** Hyder, AK — reached entirely by road via the Cassiar Highway, no ferry needed. Salmon Glacier + grizzly viewing at Fish Creek.
- **Trip end:** Ketchikan, AK, via the AMHS ferry from Prince Rupert, BC (~6 hr crossing)
